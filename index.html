Epidemy

Legend

  • Blue dot - the vaccinated
  • Yellow dot - the unvaccinated
  • Red dot - the infected

    • The model: the field is divided into square cells (think chessboard), each containing, by default, ten people. Every day, people from every cell "kiss", or have an opportunity to infect, everyone in their own cell and everyone in the neighboring cells (up to four: above, below, left and right - so, for a white field on the chessboard, that would be the nearest black fields).

    An infected person's contagious period starts the next day after being infected, and lasts exactly, invariably, the given number of days.

    The model assumes that the probability of an infection is constant. It's being calculated based on the R0, the cell size and the length of infectious period.

Disclaimer

This is a toy. Or, in other words, a very simplified model, and you should treat is as such.
